Title: Background of Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austin is the second novel of the English author. It was published in 1813 during the Regency era. There has been a 1995 TV adaptaion and many movie adaptations of the famous novel. It's a well-loved story revolving around class systems, family realtions, and pride.
